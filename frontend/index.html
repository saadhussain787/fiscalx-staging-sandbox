CRA Tax & Benefit Calendar

Critical Deadlines & Government Benefit Schedules

Stay fully aligned with Canadian Revenue Agency guidelines. Monitor filings, deadlines, and direct-deposit dates.

Corporate Tax & Payroll

Essential milestones for Canadian business structures.

Corporate Tax Filing

T2 • June 30th

Due exactly 180 days (6 months) following your corporation's fiscal year-end.

GST/HST Schedules

HST • March 31st

Annual: File & Pay 90 days post fiscal-end.
Quarterly: Due last day of month following quarter-end.

Payroll Remittances

REM • 15th Monthly

Source deductions are due to the Receiver General on the 15th of each subsequent month.

Individual & Self-Employed

Key timelines for personal earnings and claims.

RRSP Contributions

RRSP • Feb 28th

Last calendar day to submit contributions to your RRSP portfolio for previous tax year credit eligibility.

T4 and T5 Slips

SLIPS • Feb 28th

Corporate entities must file and distribute employee slips (T4 and T5) directly to the CRA.

Personal Tax (T1)

T1 • Apr 30th

Federal deadline for standard individual return filings and the payment of any outstanding balances owed.

Self-Employed (T1)

SELF • June 15th

Tax return filing is extended to June 15th; however, all outstanding balances remain due by April 30th.

CRA Government Benefits

Disbursement dates for regular federal and provincial credits.

Child Benefit (CCB)

CCB • 19th

Dispatched monthly on the 19th of each month.

Trillium Benefit (OTB)

OTB • 10th

Provincial incentive disbursements processed on the 10th day of each month.

Workers Benefit (ACWB)

ACWB • Quarterly

Distributed quarterly to qualifying workers. Standard payouts run on July 12, Oct 12, and Jan 12.

Grocery Rebate Benefit

REBATE • Quarterly

Anti-inflation offsets distributed directly by the CRA on July 5, Oct 5, Jan 5, and Apr 5.
